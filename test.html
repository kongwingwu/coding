For \(x,n\in\mathbb{N}^*\), there is

\begin{align} f\left(n,\,x\right) &=\sum_{i_1=1}^x\left(\sum_{i_2=1}^{i_1}\left(\cdots\sum_{i_n=1}^{i_{n-1}}\left(i_n\right)\right)\right)\\ &=\frac{\left(n+x\right)!}{\left(n+1\right)!\left(x-1\right)!}\\ &=C_{n+x}^{n+1}. \end{align}

And for \(x,\,n\in\mathbb{N}^*,\,n\geqslant3\), there is

\begin{align} g\left(n,\,x\right) &=\prod_{i_1=1}^x\left(\prod_{i_2=1}^{i_1}\left(\cdots\prod_{i_n=1}^{i_{n-1}}\left(i_n\right)\right)\right)\\ &=\prod_{i=1}^xi^{f\left(n-2,x+1-i\right)}\\ &=\prod_{i=1}^xi^{C_{n+x-1-i}^{n-1}}. \end{align}

Specially, there is \[g(1,\,x)=x!\quad\text{and}\quad{g(2,\,x)}=\prod_{i=1}^xi^{x+1-i}=\prod_{i=1}^xx!.\]
